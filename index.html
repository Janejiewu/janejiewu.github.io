About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant in MMLab@NTU supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy and Prof. Shangchen Zhou. I obtained M.S. degree in Signal Processing in Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Chongyi Li. Before this, I obtained B.S. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Tianjin University in 2022.

I am interested in computational photography and image editing. My current research mainly focus on object insertion and removal.

I am actively seeking PhD positions starting in 2026, with research interests in computational photography, object manipulation, and image editing. For potential opportunities, please contact me at jixin.zhao@ntu.edu.sg.
